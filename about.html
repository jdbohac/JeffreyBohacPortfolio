Jeffrey Bohac was born in Thibodaux, LA
but has spent most of his life living in the greater Atlanta metropolitain area.
He has a fun loving carefree spirit, and a great sense of humor.
When he's not cracking jokes, his nose is to the grindstone perfecting his craft.

Before learning the Fabrication trade at age 21 Jeffrey worked briefly in retail and attended the Kennesaw State University music program with a concentration in opera.

Jeffrey worked as a fabricator of steel fixtures for 8 years at Calhoun Design and Metalworks. He has crafted custom steel doors, windows, handrails, gates and other various interiors.

In February 2023 Jeffrey started attending a General Assembly Software Engineering Immersive Cohort and plans to graduate in May of this year.
He is very excited for the opportunities this new career path will present.
